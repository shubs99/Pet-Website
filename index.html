Who is Pochi ?

Pochi is an American kitten. Her breed is american short hair. Her life span is 15-17 yrs. She is very playful, active , curious and easy going.American Shorthair cats with normal origin, with middle-sized appearance, and coming from less-known breeders are priced between $500 and $800. However, An American Shorthair kitten purchased from a reputable breeder can range from $1000 -$1500 or more.
